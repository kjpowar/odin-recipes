California Pizza

Summer time pizza inspried by California style

This delicious pizza not only taste and looks like a feel of california, but its aroma is out of this world.

ingredients

  • Dough
  • Chicken
  • Cranberry Sauce

How to cook

  1. Put the dough on the tray and add sauce
  2. Put on toppings
  3. Put in oven on 180 deree for 20 mins or till done
